About Adams County

Adams County is located northeast of Denver and is one of the original counties established in Colorado. The county encompasses a range of communities with different housing types, price points, and proximity to the Denver metro core.

Buyers exploring Adams County often consider communities like Commerce City, Brighton, and Thornton as part of their search. Each community has its own character and market dynamics that buyers should research independently.

Adams County at a Glance

Adams County is served by several school districts depending on the specific community. Buyers should independently research school boundaries and district information relevant to their family's needs.

The county has seen development activity in recent years, with new construction available in some communities. Buyers interested in new construction should research current builder activity and available communities.

Adams County is connected to Denver via I-76, I-270, and other major corridors. Commute times and conditions vary by community and destination. Buyers should independently research commute routes relevant to their situation.

The Rocky Mountain Arsenal National Wildlife Refuge is located within Adams County and serves as a notable civic and natural landmark in the area.
