Relocating to Schriever Space Force Base

Practical real estate guidance for buyers relocating to Schriever Space Force Base east of Colorado Springs. Local market orientation built for military timelines.

What Buyers Should Know Before Searching

Schriever Space Force Base buyers face compressed timelines and an unfamiliar market. Understanding the basics before you start searching gives you a meaningful advantage.

Location & Commute Context

Schriever Space Force Base sits east of Colorado Springs, making Falcon and Peyton natural choices for buyers who want shorter commutes. East Colorado Springs neighborhoods also offer reasonable access without the rural character of Falcon.

Housing Market Overview

Falcon and Peyton offer newer construction with more space and suburban character. East Colorado Springs provides more established neighborhoods at a range of price points. Both areas have seen active demand.

Planning for PCS Timing

PCS moves often compress the homebuying process. Starting your market orientation early — even before orders are finalized — gives you a significant advantage in a competitive market.

Communities Near Schriever Space Force Base

Buyers relocating to Schriever Space Force Base typically consider communities east of Colorado Springs and surrounding areas for manageable commutes.

Falcon

Directly east of Colorado Springs with newer construction and suburban character; closest to Schriever.

Peyton

Further east with more rural character, larger lots, and newer development.

East Colorado Springs

Established neighborhoods with more urban amenities; slightly longer commute to Schriever.

Central Colorado Springs

More urban options with proximity to amenities; longer commute but broader lifestyle options.

Security-Widefield

South of the city, reasonable access to Schriever and Fort Carson.

Fountain

Affordable options south of Colorado Springs with reasonable access to east-side installations.

Community descriptions are general orientation only. Commute times, pricing, and inventory vary. Confirm current conditions with Rob before making decisions.
