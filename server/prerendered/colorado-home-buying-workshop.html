Programs Covered

Discover What You May Qualify For

Low Down Payment Options

Learn about low down payment and, for qualified buyers, no down payment loan options that may be available.

VA Advantage

Eligible active duty service members, veterans, and surviving spouses may qualify for VA financing benefits, including low or no down payment options.

FHA + Assistance Programs

See how FHA financing and Colorado assistance programs may help reduce upfront costs for qualified buyers.

Conventional Options

Explore flexible conventional loan options that may work for qualified buyers with lower down payments.

Renovation Financing

Understand how some buyers use renovation financing to purchase a home and fund eligible improvements.

Seller Concessions Strategy

Learn how seller concessions may help reduce upfront closing costs. In some transactions, Rob Baker has negotiated more than $20,000 in concessions, though results vary by property, financing, market conditions, and terms.
